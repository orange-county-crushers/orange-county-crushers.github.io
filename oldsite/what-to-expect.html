What To Expect: Travel Baseball


TIME: To prepare for the start of the season, each team will practice 2-3 times per week on weeknights and weekends. As the season starts, 1 weekday practice and weekend practices when the team is not playing is normal. Each team plays around 8-10 tournaments a season over 3 months. Tournaments are Saturday/Sunday all day events.


COMMITMENT: All travel baseball teams have more players interested than playing spots. Travel teams have smaller rosters to minimize bench time. To get the most out of our program 100% practice and tournament attendance is expected. All other sporting commitments are secondary given the investment by the entire team.


GROWTH: To the best of our abilities, the Crusher Organization has tried to adopt the Philosophy outlined by Mike Matheny, St. Louis Cardinals Manager.  Given the excellent youth instruction provided by Coach Patrick, Parent Coaches are able to focus on the Team, not the mechanics. We highly recommend parents read the Manifesto no matter what team or sport your child plays.
