High School Development Program


The primary goal of all of our Teams is to produce solid, fundamentally sound baseball players. We offer Pre-Season training programs for our High School Players to get them ready for their team tryouts and seasons. This Development program is led by Coach Currin and is designed to promote proper baseball fundamentals, safe throwing mechanics, and overall athleticism.. 


Coach Currin offers private year round coaching to all Crushers including Pitching, Catching, Fielding and Hitting Instruction.
