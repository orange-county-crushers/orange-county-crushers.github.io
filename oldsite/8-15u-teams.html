The goal of all of our Teams is to develop fundamentally sound high school players. Our Youth Development program is developed by Coach Currin and designed to develop solid individual and team fundamentals. 


Coach Currin offers private year round coaching to all Crushers including Pitching, Catching, Fielding and Hitting Instruction.

Tryout Information 13U - 14U


Please send a message using the For More Info Page or directly to occ11u@outlook.com. All Advanced Teams will begin playing by early May. Practices will start in April. Tryouts will be done in regular practices.


Our 15U Team is currently full and does not expect to have any openings this Spring. If you need assistance finding a team, contact us as we can put you in touch with other area teams.
